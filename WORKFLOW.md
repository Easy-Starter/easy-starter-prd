# Workflow: Idea to Launch

## 0. Intake

Artifacts: **Opportunity assessment, Product One-Pager, business-model hypothesis, assumptions log, open questions**

Exit: **problem, target user, desired outcome, business hypothesis, and unknowns are visible.**

## 1. Discovery

Artifacts: **research plan, interview notes, desk research, alternatives analysis, personas/JTBD, user journey, validation experiments**

Exit: **user pains are evidence-backed or marked as assumptions.**

## 2. Define

Artifacts: **PRD, scope, success metrics, prioritization**

Exit: **MVP scope and acceptance criteria are testable.**

## 3. Design

Artifacts: **Design Brief, IA, flows, UX/UI spec, Figma file plan, prototype, usability plan**

Exit: **happy path, edge cases, empty/loading/error states, responsive behavior, and accessibility are covered.**

## 4. Technical Planning

Artifacts: **Technical Brief, API/Data Spec, Security/Privacy Brief, Feature Spec, Plan**

Exit: **architecture decisions, contracts, data model, tests, and rollback path are clear.**

## 5. Tasking

Artifacts: **tasks.md and QA plan**

Exit: **small ordered tasks, test-first where possible, with requirement IDs.**

## 6. Implementation

Artifacts: **branches, PRs, tests, changelog**

Exit: **tests pass and PRs cite requirement IDs.**

## 7. Launch

Artifacts: **release plan, GTM plan, analytics, support docs, rollback plan**

Exit: **release is measurable and reversible.**

## 8. Learn

Artifacts: **metrics report, feedback synthesis, decisions, updated specs**

Exit: **learning becomes product decisions.**
