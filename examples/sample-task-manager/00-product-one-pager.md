# Product One-Pager: Team Task Manager

## Idea
A simple web app for small teams to create projects, assign tasks, and move tasks across a Kanban board.

## Problem
Small teams track work in chats/spreadsheets, making ownership and blockers hard to see.

## Target Users
| ID | Segment | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| USER-001 | Small product/software teams with 3-10 people | High |

## MVP
- Create project
- Create task
- Assign owner
- Move task between To Do, In Progress, Review, Done

## Success Metric
| ID | Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| METRIC-001 | Weekly active teams | 10 in first 30 days |
